All about me

I am an entry level designer and junior software programmer with a multitude of small projects. I've spent a considerable amount of time tinkering with languages such as C++, Python, JavaScript, HTML and CSS, whilst studying for my MComp at USW (University South Wales). Although, I'd like to say I'm competent but I always find myself learning something new that makes me look like a complete newbie or out of my depth (the beauty of evolving technology right?)

For the past year I have been working as a Junior Software Engineer, definitely not the most exciting aspect of the industry but a valuable one none the less! You see, as you leave university expecting to be the next big thing you essentially fall into the industry and get knocked back a peg or two by some form of standard or technology that throws a tsunami of pre-dinosaur code your way that is only decipherable by the gods themselves.

But alas, what doesn't kill you makes you stronger (or so I've heard)
